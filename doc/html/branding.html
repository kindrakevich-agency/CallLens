01 Logo

The mark — a sound wave

The logo is made up of the mark — an animated sound wave — and the CallLens wordmark set in Space Grotesk Bold. The wave represents a live call being turned into data.

What not to do
  • ✕don't stretch or skew the mark
  • ✕don't change the number of bars
  • ✕don't place it on a busy background
  • ✕don't recolor it in off-brand colors
